F.A.Q's

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Frequently Asked Questions here.

How to view profile?

Click on the organization name in the top right corner and select view profile.

How to upgrade membership?

In the navigation bar click on upgrade membership and select the upgrade based on the needs of the organization. Contact us for further questions regarding membership.

How to add users?

Click the agency name in the top right corner and select manage users to add, delete and edit user permissions.

Can I share this opportunities on another site?

Yes. In order to respond to the opportunity, users must create a profile.

Is there training on how to use the platform?

Yes. Go to support in the navigation bar and view training.
